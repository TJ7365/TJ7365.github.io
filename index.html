Quant Strategy Pipeline

End-to-end quantitative trading pipeline that backtests an EMA crossover + ATR-based risk management strategy on live equity data, persists results to a SQLite database, and visualizes performance through an interactive dashboard.

PythonSQLiteStreamlitPandasBacktesting
Key Achievements
  • Built a modular backtest engine with EMA crossover signals, ATR-based position sizing, and VIX regime filtering on SPY data from 2015–present
  • Engineered a SQLite persistence layer storing runs, trades, equity curves, and metrics — enabling multi-run comparison and leaderboard ranking by Sharpe ratio

VolEdge – Options Pricing & Risk Dashboard

↗

An interactive quantitative dashboard that calculates the fair value of options contracts (the right to buy or sell a stock at a set price) and measures risk exposure in real time — combining three industry-standard models used by professional traders and risk analysts at top financial institutions.

PythonBlack-ScholesGARCHMonte CarloOptions PricingRisk Analytics
Key Achievements
  • Implemented Black-Scholes pricing model to calculate theoretically fair option prices and Greeks (Delta, Gamma, Vega, Theta) — the sensitivity metrics traders use to manage directional and volatility risk
  • Integrated GARCH(1,1) volatility forecasting to dynamically estimate how much a stock's price is expected to swing going forward, producing a more accurate risk input than static historical averages
  • Built a Monte Carlo simulation engine running thousands of randomized price-path scenarios to stress-test option value under different market conditions — mirroring risk frameworks used in institutional derivatives desks
